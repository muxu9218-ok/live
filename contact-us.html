Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting our Quran academy

What is the best way to contact the Shia Quran academy?

For quick responses about our online Quran classes, we recommend using WhatsApp or our contact form. For detailed inquiries about Quran courses or admissions, WhatsApp is preferred. Our phone lines are available 24/7 online support for immediate assistance with Quran education.

How long does it take to get a response about Quran courses?

We strive to respond to all inquiries about our Shia Quran Academy within 2 hours. WhatsApp messages typically receive responses within a few hours during working hours. Emails are answered within 2-4 hours for all Quran education inquiries.

Do you offer support in languages other than English for Quran learning?

Yes, our support team at Shia Online Quran Academy can assist you in English, Arabic, Urdu, and Farsi. Please specify your preferred language when contacting us for online Quran classes.

Can I schedule a meeting with a Shia teacher or administrator?

Absolutely! You can request a meeting through our contact form or by calling our administration at Shia Quran Academy. We offer virtual meetings via Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or other platforms for your convenience with our qualified teachers.

Is there a dedicated contact for technical issues during online Quran classes?

Yes, we have a dedicated technical support team available during class hours at our online Quran academy. You can reach them at support@shiaquranacademy.com or through our WhatsApp technical support line for assistance with Quran education platforms.
