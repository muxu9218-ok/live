Comprehensive Qaida Learning Curriculum

Structured program designed for beginners to learn Quran reading properly

Step-by-Step Learning Path

Foundation Level (Weeks 1-8)

  • Arabic Alphabet Recognition & Sounds
  • Harakat (Short Vowels) - Fatha, Kasra, Damma
  • Tanween (Nunation) - Double Vowels
  • Huroof al-Madd (Letters of Elongation)
  • Sukoon (Jazm) - Absence of Vowel
  • Shaddah (Tashdeed) - Letter Stress
  • Joining Letters Practice
  • Basic Reading Exercises

Intermediate Level (Weeks 9-16)

  • Rules of Noon Sakinah & Tanween
  • Rules of Meem Sakinah
  • Madd Rules (Natural, Compulsory)
  • Qalqalah Letters & Rules
  • Heavy and Light Letters
  • Basic Stopping Rules (Waqf)
  • Recitation of Short Surahs
  • Practice with Shia Duas
