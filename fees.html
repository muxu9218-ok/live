Fees & Payment FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our fee structure and payment options

What is included in the course fees?

Our Shia Quran Academy fees include all learning materials, one-on-one sessions with qualified teachers, progress tracking, and certificates upon completion (for eligible plans). There are no hidden charges or additional costs.

Are there any registration or enrollment fees?

No, we do not charge any registration or enrollment fees. The prices displayed are all-inclusive for the selected plan with transparent pricing.

Can I change my payment plan after enrollment?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your Quran learning plan at any time. The changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle with appropriate prorated charges or credits.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a full refund within the first 7 days of enrollment if you're not satisfied with our services. After that period, refunds are provided on a prorated basis for unused portions of your Quran course plan.

Do you offer discounts for multiple students from the same family?

Yes, we offer a 15% family discount for the second student and 25% discount for the third and subsequent students from the same family enrolled in our Shia Quran programs.

Are your Quran course fees competitive with other online academies?

Yes, our Shia Quran Academy pricing is highly competitive while maintaining the highest quality of education. We offer premium Shia Quran instruction at affordable rates with the added benefit of authentic Shia methodology.
