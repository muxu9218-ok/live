Our Memorization Process

Structured approach to ensure successful Quran memorization

1

New Lesson (Sabaq)

Daily new memorization with proper Tajweed and correct pronunciation under teacher guidance.

2

Recent Revision (Dhor)

Revision of previously memorized portions from last 30 days to strengthen retention.

3

Old Revision (Paras/Months)

Systematic revision of older memorized portions to prevent forgetting.

4

Testing & Certification

Regular testing and final certification with Ijazah upon completion.
