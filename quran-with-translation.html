Tafsir Explanation
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ ﴿١﴾ الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ ﴿٢﴾ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ ﴿٣﴾

- English Quran Translation by S.V. Mir Ahmed Ali

شروع اللہ کے نام سے جو بڑا مہربان نہایت رحم واﻻ ہے (1)

سب تعریف اللہ ہی کے لئے ہے جو تمام جہانوں کا پروردگار ہے (2)

بڑا مہربان نہایت رحم واﻻ ہے (3)

- اردو قرآن ترجمہ از سید علی نقی نقوی

According to Shia Tafsir of Ahlulbayt, "Bismillah" is not just a phrase but contains the essence of Allah's attributes. The repetition of "Al-Rahman" and "Al-Raheem" emphasizes Allah's comprehensive and specific mercy for all creation and believers respectively.

Imam Ali (A.S) has explained that "Alhamdulillah" encompasses all forms of praise and gratitude, recognizing that every blessing originates from Allah alone, the sustainer of all worlds. This Quran interpretation helps in deeper Quran understanding.

- Based on Tafsir Al-Mizan and Tafsir Namoona
