Dua Kumayl - Supplication of Imam Ali (A.S)

Recited on Thursday nights for forgiveness and spiritual elevation

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ بِرَحْمَتِكَ الَّتِي وَسِعَتْ كُلَّ شَيْءٍ

"In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful. O Allah, I ask You by Your mercy which embraces all things."

About This Dua

Dua Kumayl is one of the most famous and powerful supplications taught by Imam Ali (A.S) to his companion Kumayl ibn Ziyad. It is traditionally recited on Thursday nights or during the holy month of Ramadan. This Dua seeks forgiveness of sins, protection from evil, and spiritual purification.

The Dua emphasizes Allah's infinite mercy and the believer's complete dependence on divine grace. It contains profound theological concepts and serves as a comprehensive prayer for both worldly and spiritual needs.
